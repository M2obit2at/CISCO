Description

I am Thato Mothobi, As a passionate web developer, I specialize in creating dynamic and responsive websites that provide an exceptional user experience. With a strong foundation in HTML, CSS,and JavaScript, I bring innovative solutions to life, from concept to deployment. My portfolio showcases a diverse range of projects, highlighting my skills in front-end, as well as my commitment to delivering high-quality, efficient, and scalable web applications.

2022

In 2022, I embarked on a journey to pursue my dream of a career in IT by enrolling in a degree in IT in Business Systems at Rosebank. This opportunity allowed me to delve into the world of software development, which I quickly grew passionate about. Despite my enthusiasm and dedication, I was unable to complete the program due to financial constraints, after having studied for one year. However, the experience solidified my commitment to the field of IT and has driven me to continue learning and growing as a web developer.

2023

In 2023, I embarked on a programming learnership with CTU Training Solutions. This 12-month program was divided into two parts: the first six months focused on learning and building a strong foundation in programming, while the latter six months were dedicated to practical training in a workplace environment. I thoroughly enjoyed this experience and gained valuable knowledge and skills that further fueled my passion for software development.

2024

After completing my programming certificate, I enrolled in a Diploma in Information Technology (Software Development) to build upon my existing knowledge. Simultaneously, I began working remotely for Codsoft as a web developer, allowing me to apply my skills in a professional setting while continuing to expand my expertise in software development.
