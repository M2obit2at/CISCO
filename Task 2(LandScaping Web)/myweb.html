What we do

Basic Cleaning Package

Our basic cleaning package includes surface cleaning, dusting, vacuuming, and mopping. We also clean kitchen surfaces, bathrooms, and common areas. Ideal for regular maintenance.

Deep Cleaning Package

Our deep cleaning package offers a thorough cleaning of all surfaces, including hard-to-reach areas. We clean inside cabinets, behind appliances, baseboards, and light fixtures. Perfect for move-in/move-out cleaning or seasonal cleaning.

Office Cleaning Package

Our office cleaning package focuses on creating a clean and hygienic workspace. We clean desks, chairs, conference rooms, break rooms, and sanitize high-touch surfaces. Customizable to fit your office's needs and schedule.

Eco-Friendly Cleaning

Our eco-friendly cleaning package utilizes environmentally friendly cleaning products and methods to minimize our impact on the environment. We prioritize green cleaning solutions while still delivering effective results.

Premium Cleaning Package

Our premium cleaning package is a comprehensive cleaning solution that includes all the services from our other packages, plus additional specialized cleaning services such as window cleaning, refrigerator cleaning, and oven cleaning. Perfect for special occasions or for those who want the ultimate cleaning experience.
